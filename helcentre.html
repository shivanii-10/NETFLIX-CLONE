Here are some common questions and their answers to help you:

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the sign-in page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How do I change my subscription plan?

To change your subscription plan, go to "Settings" and then select "Subscription Plan" to change your current plan.

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues, here are some steps you can take:

  • Make sure your device is compatible with our service.
